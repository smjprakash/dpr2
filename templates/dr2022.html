AWS DeepRacer League - India 2021


The AWS DeepRacer League in India aimed to bring together students to learn collaboratively and get hands on with machine learning through a cloud-based virtual racing simulator. After going through rigorous regional and national round of racing, top 15 students from the country were selected to race in the grand finale of ​AWS DeepRacer League - India 2022.

Shaili Chopra is the founder of SheThePeople.TV, India’s biggest platform for real stories of real women. She is a Stanford Draper Hills Fellow and was named Impact's 40 Under 40 in 2020. In April 2017, she received the prestigious Vital Voices fellowship, instituted by Hillary Clinton and Madeleine Albright.

Shaili has a prolific journalism career, which has now furthered her into entrepreneurship, allowing her to use her news skills to create meaningful content for the web. In her last role, she was the Senior Editor and Lead Anchor at ET NOW, Economic Times Business News Channel and earlier with India’s largest tv network NDTV. In March 2016 Shaili was announced one of India’s Top 50 Most Influential Women in Media, Marketing and Advertising by IMPACT Magazine.

Shaili received India’s highest honour in journalism, the Ramnath Goenka Award for Best in Business Journalism. She also won the Media Foundation Best Business Anchor Award for 2010 and, in the same year, the Federation of Indian Industry (FICCI) gave her the Young Women’s Achiever Award for her contribution to media. She also won the News Television Award for the Best Reporter in India in 2007.

Prof. K VijayRaghavan

Principal Scientific Adviser to the Government of India

View Profile

Prof K. VijayRaghavan is a distinguished professor in the field of developmental genetics and former director of the National Centre of Biological Sciences. His fields of specialization are developmental biology, genetics, and neurogenetics.

Education

Prof. VijayRaghavan graduated with a Bachelor of Technology degree in Chemical Engineering from IIT Kanpur in 1975. He completed his doctoral work in 1983 in the field of Molecular Biology and holds a PhD from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research

Initial Career

Worked as a Senior Research Fellow at the California Institute of Technology, after which he joined the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, where he was instrumental in establishing the National Centre for Biological Sciences (NCBS), Bengaluru, under the aegis of Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in 1992.

Groundbreaking Research Work

He spearheaded groundbreaking research to understand the important principles and mechanisms that control the nervous system and muscles during development, and how these neuromuscular systems direct specific locomotor behaviours.

Professional Details
Principal Scientific Advisor to the Government of India since 2018, he has served as Director of the National Centre for Biological Sciences, Bengaluru, where he continues as a Distinguished Professor. He was Secretary, Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India between January 2013 to January 2018.
